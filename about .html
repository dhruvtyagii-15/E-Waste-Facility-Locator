About Us

The E-Waste Facility Locator is a platform dedicated to helping individuals and businesses responsibly dispose of their electronic waste. Our mission is to provide easy access to recycling facilities where users can safely dispose of their old electronics.

We believe in the importance of reducing electronic waste and protecting the environment. By promoting proper disposal methods, we aim to minimize the negative impact of e-waste on our planet.

Our Goals

  • Make e-waste recycling convenient and accessible to everyone.
  • Educate the public about the importance of responsible electronic waste disposal.
  • Partner with recycling facilities to expand our network and reach more communities.
